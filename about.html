About Spooky Popups

We aim to bring our customers top quality Halloween gear at a low price, we can't be bothered with a store all year long when all you need us for is 1 month. To help keep costs low we'll take over any abandoned or vacant space starting on October 1st for just over a month to bring our customers the Halloween gear they need, when they need it, for amazing prices.

We keep our costs low and pass those onto the customers! When you shop with us you can be sure that the spooky deals you see are the best around! No need to shop around, we've done that for you and rest assured you won't find our goods at a better price anywhere! We are family friendly! Halloween is for everyone! From young children to older adults, our store stocks great spooky products to meet all ages!

Because we know our Halloween offerings are the best around in both quality and price, every sale is final.
